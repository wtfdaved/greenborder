About The Green Border

The Green Border was built for the 915 community — the El Paso residents, the Sunland Park locals, and the border-crossers navigating two states with very different cannabis laws.

We cut through the noise to give you accurate, up-to-date dispensary info, legal guidance, and honest cannabis journalism rooted in the unique culture of the US-Mexico border region.

No corporate affiliations. No paid rankings. Just a community resource for the people who live here.
